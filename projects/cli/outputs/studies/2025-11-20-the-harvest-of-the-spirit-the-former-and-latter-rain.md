## Section 1: The Agricultural Context

To interpret the Bible correctly, we must first understand what these terms meant to the people who heard them. In Palestine, there were two critical rainy seasons essential for the harvest.

1.  **The Former (Early) Rain:** This fell in the autumn (October/November) at seed-sowing time. Its purpose was to germinate the seed and help it take root. Without this, there would be no life.
2.  **The Latter Rain:** This fell in the spring (March/April) just before the harvest. Its purpose was to ripen the grain, preparing it for the sickle.

- **The Former Rain** represents the outpouring of the Holy Spirit at the beginning of the Christian dispensation (Pentecost) and, individually, at the beginning of our Christian walk (Conversion).
- **The Latter Rain** represents the final outpouring of the Holy Spirit to finish Godâ€™s work on earth and to ripen the character of the believer for heaven.
