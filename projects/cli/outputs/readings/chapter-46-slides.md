Slide 2 Biblical Answer: “And he said unto me, Unto two thousand and three hundred days; then shall the sanctuary be cleansed.” (Daniel 8:14)

Slide 3 Explanation: This is the longest time prophecy in the Bible. While Daniel saw symbols of earthly kingdoms (the ram and the goat), the timing of the sanctuary's cleansing was the central mystery.

Slide 4 Insight: The Day-Year Principle

[DYK] In Bible prophecy, a "day" often represents a literal year (Ezekiel 4:6, Numbers 14:34). Therefore, 2300 prophetic days equal 2300 literal years.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of an ancient scribe’s hand carefully marking a calendar or timeline on parchment, symbolizing the counting of years, soft golden lighting.

Slide 7 Explanation: Daniel understood the symbols of the nations—Media-Persia and Greece—because Gabriel explained them. However, the timeline of the "evening and morning" (the 2300 days) remained a mystery to him, causing him physical distress.

Slide 10 Progressive Disclosure: Connecting the Visions

1.  **The Connection**: Daniel 8 ends with a lack of understanding. Daniel 9 begins with prayer.
2.  **The Messenger**: The same angel, Gabriel, returns specifically to explain the "vision" from the previous chapter.
3.  **The Key**: The explanation of the "70 weeks" in Daniel 9 is the key to unlocking the starting point of the "2300 days."

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of two scrolls side by side; one is rolled up and dark, the other is open and glowing, symbolizing the unlocking of a mystery.

---

Slide 11 Term Definition: "Determined"

The Hebrew word used in Daniel 9:24 is *chathak*, which literally means "cut off." The 70 weeks (490 years) were "cut off" from the longer 2300-year period and allotted specifically to the Jewish people and Jerusalem.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a pair of hands measuring a long piece of fabric and cutting a smaller section from the beginning of it, soft historical setting.

---

Slide 12 Question: When does this great prophetic clock begin ticking?

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a large, ancient sundial in a city square, with a shadow casting a precise line, symbolizing the start of a specific time.

---

Slide 13 Biblical Answer: “Know therefore and understand, that from the going forth of the commandment to restore and to build Jerusalem unto the Messiah the Prince...” (Daniel 9:25)

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a Persian King handing a royal decree to a scribe or priest, symbolizing the command to rebuild, historically respectful.

---

Slide 14 Explanation: The Starting Point

The starting gun for this prophecy is the decree to restore Jerusalem. According to Ezra 7, this decree was finalized in the seventh year of Artaxerxes, established by historians as **457 B.C.**

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of construction workers in ancient times laying stones to rebuild the walls of Jerusalem, bathed in the warm light of dawn.

---

Slide 15 Illustration: The Timeline

[ILL] Imagine a long measuring tape of 2300 inches. If you cut off the first 490 inches, both sections must start at the exact same point. If the 490 years began in 457 B.C., then the 2300 years also began in 457 B.C.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a carpenter’s workbench with measuring tools and a long measuring rod laid out, highlighting the starting point, soft atmospheric lighting.

---

Slide 16 Question: How does this prophecy accurately predict the arrival of Jesus?

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a crowd gathering at the banks of the Jordan River, looking expectantly toward a figure in the water.

---

Slide 17 Biblical Answer: “...unto the Messiah the Prince shall be seven weeks, and threescore and two weeks...” (Daniel 9:25)

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of an ancient scroll with Hebrew text, the words glowing slightly, pointing toward a vision of a crown or scepter.

---

Slide 18 Explanation: The Arrival of the Messiah

The prophecy states that 483 years would pass from the decree (457 B.C.) until the Messiah.
*   483 years minus 457 B.C. brings us to **27 A.D.**
*   In 27 A.D., Jesus was baptized and anointed by the Holy Spirit. "Messiah" means "The Anointed One."

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of Jesus standing in the Jordan River, a dove descending upon Him with rays of light breaking through the clouds.

---

Slide 19 Speaker Note: Spirit-led Tangent

[SN] It is profound that the Jewish leaders of Christ’s day knew the Scriptures but missed the time of their visitation. The prophecy was mathematically precise—pointing to the exact year of His baptism—yet it was humble fishermen and foreigners who often recognized Him first.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a group of humble fishermen mending nets by the sea, looking up with expressions of hope and recognition.

---

Slide 20 Question: What was predicted to happen in the "midst" (middle) of the final week of the 490 years?

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a storm gathering over Jerusalem, the lighting dramatic and solemn, foreshadowing a momentous event.

Slide 21 Biblical Answer: “And He shall confirm the covenant with many for one week: and in the midst of the week He shall cause the sacrifice and the oblation to cease.” (Daniel 9:27)

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of the temple curtain tearing from top to bottom, revealing the Most Holy Place, soft dusty light in the sanctuary.

Slide 22 Explanation: The Crucifixion

The "midst" of the seven-year week is 3½ years.
*   Jesus’ ministry began in 27 A.D.
*   Exactly 3½ years later, in the spring of **31 A.D.**, Jesus was crucified.
*   His death made earthly sacrifices unnecessary.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of three crosses on a distant hill at sunset, silhouette against a dark sky, symbolizing the final sacrifice.

---

Slide 23 Question: When does the entire 2300-year period end?

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of an hourglass with the sand nearly run out, sitting on a table alongside an open Bible, soft contemplative light.

---

Slide 24 The Mathematical Conclusion

1.  The 490 years ended in **34 A.D.** (The Gospel to the Gentiles).
2.  Take the total 2300 years and subtract the first 490 years = 1810 years remaining.
3.  34 A.D. + 1810 years = **1844 A.D.**

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of a timeline drawn on parchment, showing the progression from the cross to a date in the modern era, historically respectful style.

---

Slide 25 Deeper Insight: The Cleansing of the Sanctuary

The angel said, "then shall the sanctuary be cleansed." Since the earthly sanctuary was destroyed, this refers to the sanctuary in heaven. In 1844, Jesus began the final phase of His high priestly work—the Investigative Judgment.

[IMG] Warm classical biblical painting of Jesus as the High Priest standing before a golden ark in a glorious heavenly temple, surrounded by divine light and angels.

---

This prophecy proves two things:
1.  **Divine Inspiration:** God mapped out history with mathematical precision.
2.  **Solemn Times:** We are living in the time of judgment. It is a call to ask, "Is my life hid with Christ in God?"
