Chapter 105
The Two Resurrections
=====================

Because of the Fall, what comes to every man? “In Adam all die." 1 Corinthians
15:22. (See also Romans 5:12.)
In what condition is man while in death? “Whatsoever thy hand findeth to do, do
it with thy might; for there is no work, nor device, nor knowledge, nor wisdom, in the
grave, whither thou goest." Ecclesiastes 9:10.
NOTE. - That is, man, when dead, has no use of the powers of mind or body. He cannot, therefore,
while in the grave, praise God, or even think of Him (Psalm 6:5); for in the day he dies his thoughts perish.
